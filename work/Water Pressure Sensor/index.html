water_pressure_sensor.proj
Water Pressure Sensor

This project explored methods of sealing electronics for underwater use while maintaining reliable sensor readings. Most importantly, we were constrained to a total BOM cost of 25$. Multiple enclosure iterations were tested to balance robustness, manufacturability, and signal integrity under increasing pressure.
