Rocket Test Stand

This project analyzed the thrust imparted by a hobby rocket engine through Arduino and a load cell. My team designed a rigid 8020 frame that used bearings on a fulcrum to impart a pulling force on the load cell. My contributions to the project included designing and printing the fulcrum mechanism and casing. I designed two hemispherical 8020 adapters which I then “welded” together with a 3D printing pen. Use of ABS filament made this configuration sufficiently strong for our project’s purposes.
