The Getaway Mobile Smart Home
2022–2023 Academic Year

For our Senior Design project, 35 of my Mechanical Engineering classmates built a self-sufficient Mobile Smart Home. My subteam focused on making the trailer “smart” by integrating various sensors to a Centralized Monitoring System (CMS).

The Getaway
Fall 2022

My subteam spent the semester researching and prototyping solutions to fully integrate the trailer to a wall-mounted Central Monitoring System (CMS). We sought to manage temperature, water levels, lighting (interior and exterior), music, solar panel power generation, power use, travel tracking, and more, all in one place. We discovered an open-source home management software called HomeAssistantOS and began sourcing compatible sensors.We were also tasked with creating an exterior workbench.

I presented our ideas to the professors and secured an award to double our team's funding for the next semester. We definitely needed it.

Spring 2023

With as much planning as we did last semester, putting it together in real life is going to be a breeze, right?

Integration was the million-dollar problem in the Spring. Getting our sensors to connect to our central RPi without an internet connection was its own challenge, but the real fun happened when all 35 people needed to get into the trailer to implement their features at the same time. We also learned an excellent lesson in data management when our (not backed up) RPi's SD card snapped in half a week before we presented.
