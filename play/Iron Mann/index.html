Iron Mann Mask
2025 Update!

I gave Iron Mann the overhaul it desperately needed, and I'm happy to report that the new mask is fully functional! I completely reworked the mask to rely on 2-bar linkages on both sides, rewrote the code to make it only rely on one servo, and reprinted the whole thing at a size that didn't hurt the noggin.

The Old Guts

Two servos controlled the joint angles. As I learned through this project, 3D printed dynamic joints are much less reliable than the much more refined ones on, for example, the UR5e.

guts.jpg
The outcome (Circa 2023)

The end result is, in a word, a disasterpiece. This project reminded me of the many difficult facets of integrating software and hardware (and made me wish I had a J.A.R.V.I.S.). I look forward to continuing on this project in my spare time though—be sure to check in again to see my progress!
